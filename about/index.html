About Kylee Sodomka

Kylee Sodomka is a junior double majoring in sports media and communication and broadcasting. While at UNL, she has focused on gaining experience in different parts of the sports media world. She has been a staff member of the KRNU Radio Station, a social media manager for the Cornhusker Marching Band’s Trumpets, and a PA Announcer for the Hastings Sodbusters baseball team and legion baseball. Currently, she is a camera operator for HuskerVision. After she graduates, she hopes to work for baseball and/or softball. Her ultimate goal would be to work for the Chicago Cubs.
